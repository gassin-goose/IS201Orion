What Makes Intellicare Services Different from Competitors?

Dry Time

Sick and tired of long wait times from wet floors?

Encapsulation technology from Releaseit© is a low moisture product that reduces dry time from 6+ hours to just one hour!

Wicking and Re-emerging Spots

Why does it always look dirty, even after cleaning?

Most types of commercial carpet cleaning can cause dirt and stains that have sunk beneath the visible carpet to "wick" or rise up to the top, leaving you with the same old stain just days after your service.

Intellicare services uses an encapsulation type product that will surround and trap dirt or stains in fine crystal-like structures that will be vacummed out of the carpet during routine building care. Leaving your carpet looking like new.

Better Results Over Time

Because Releaseit© causes dirt and other stains to be vacummed out of the carpet, each time you vacuum will leave the carpet looking better than the last, meaning less hastle for your business.
